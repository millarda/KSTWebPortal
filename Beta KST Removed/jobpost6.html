Receptionist/Front Desk Assistant


Ka’ala Systems Technology Corp. is in need of Receptionist/Front Desk assistant to support the Navy Housing Service Center (HCS) located at Joint Base Pearl Harbor/Hickam

The Candidate shall provide the following administrative duties:

  • Receive questions and provide answers to basic information related to transferring military families regarding family housing availability, and/or wait periods, area information, and business locations to include directions, phone numbers and/or hours of operation.
  • Refer customers to the appropriate department or individual, and greet all customers and guests upon entry to HSC.
  • Assist customers with filling out various documents such as loaner furniture request, HSC Briefing sheet, Housing Exception to Policy letters/request, and/or housing application.
  • Schedule and confirm appointments for those applying for Navy Public-Private Venture (PPV) or HSC services and ensure customers know of and bring all completed and/or required documentation to their scheduled appointment.
  • Log-in and schedule all applicants for an interview with the appropriate Customer Support Assistant.
  • Log, input, and file all Advance Applications and contact customers for any missing data.
  • Respond to email request for information related to Housing Services, area, and confirmation of appointment and/or required documentation.
  • Produce daily Customer Service Branch Schedules and provide copies to team (required no sooner than 10 minutes before closing the work day prior to within the first 10 minutes of the scheduled day) and update, as necessary, for any walk-in and/or call-in customers.
  • Update and prepare reports utilizing CNIC Enterprise Military Housing (eMH) website. All updates and reports must be completed before close of business each business day.
  • Access eMH website for schedules, housing lists, resident information and other reports.
  • Follow-up with customers to ensure they received Interactive Customer Evaluations (ICE) and encourage them to complete their ICE; retrieve completed surveys and securely store in accordance with HSC policy.
  • Provide customer service to prospective and current UH customers regarding their eligibility, rights, privileges, and responsibilities.
  • Provide assistance with Basic Allowance for Housing (BAH), Temporary Lodging Authorization (TLA) to determine authorized allowances and issues non-availability certificates.
  • Prepare documents for authorization of BAH & TLA. Provide guidance on initial and subsequent issue/turn-in of government owned loaner furnishings for private market living or inventory within assigned Government UH rooms.
  • Greet all customers and guest upon entry to UH Administrative Offices.
  • Utilize the eMH website to prepare reports and complete daily tasks.
  • Additional administrative tasks shall include, but not be limited to, data entry, data retrieval, scanning documents, copying documents, filing, and shredding.
