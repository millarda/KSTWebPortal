Systems Administrator (DLA)


Ka’ala Systems Technology Corp. is in need of Systems Administrator to support the Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) by providing IT support services for DLA personnel located at Naval Base, Guam

Candidate shall provide software and Windows Server System Administration support for all DLA systems to include software/hardware configuration, security implementation, application interfaces, troubleshooting, consultation and new workload planning to the variety of current and new customers.

IT support will consist of the following tasks:

  • Candidate shall provide support for the implementation and configuration of Global Policy Objects (GPO) within the DLA active directory domain in accordance with policies of the DLA Information Assurance Manager. The Candidate will serve as the subject matter expert and participate in monthly teleconferences involving DLA Enterprise Administrators.
  • Candidate shall provide support for the implementation and configuration of virtualization technologies including VmWare ESXi software within the environment.
  • Candidate shall provide support for implementation, configuration and management of Storage Area Network.
  • Candidate shall provide technical guidance on new workstation deployment utilizing automated methods such as a Pre-Boot Execution Environment (PXE) in conjunction with System Center Control Manager.
  • Telecommunications Support. Requires Virtual Private Networking (VPN), Router, and Switch support. The SP shall have an understanding of the devices and interrelationships of the devices as used on the LAN and WAN. The SP shall occasionally assist with network related tasks.
  • The Candidate shall provide Tier II support for users. Problems that cannot be resolved at this level shall be prioritized and elevated.

Experience:


  • Two years of experience in a related field of assignment.
  • Knowledge of DoD IT systems, to include Windows, UNIX, and comprehensive systems administration concepts.

Minimum Required Certifications:


Must currently possess applicable certifications in accordance with DoD 8570.01, IAT Level II.IAT Level II certification can be one of the following:

  • GSEC
  • Security+ CE
  • SSCP

Candidates must also possess one of the following operating systems certifications:

  • Microsoft Certified Solutions Associate (MCSA) Windows 7 or higher.

Security Clearance:


Department of Defense secret security clearance is required. Candidates selected either must currently possess an active Department of Defense Secret Clearance and maintain the secret clearance for access to classified information or closed/restricted areas throughout duration of employment.
