Mail Clerk


 Ka’ala Systems Technology Corp. is in need of Mail Support Services Clerk for Joint Base Pearl Harbor Hickam (JBPHH). Fleet Logistics Center Pearl Harbor Hawaii is the coordinator for mail services for all shore-based naval personnel and shore activities, as well as Air Force activities in the Hawaii region. Prior military experience desired.

Job Duties include:


  • Mail Processing – The Mail Clerk will receive and process mail from designated customers and ensure all mail is counted, scanned, and accounted for. Processed mail shall be put in the correct postal bin marked with appropriate Command, Department, or designator for Unaccompanied Housing (UH) barracks as appropriate. Mail shall be processed within one workday of receipt in order to meet delivery standards.
  • Quality Assurance (QA) Process – The Mail Clerk shall ensure mail meets DoD standards outlined in DoD 4525.8M. If mail does not meet postal standards, it shall be returned to the customer and a discrepancy explanation shall be provided.
  • Mail Distribution – The Mail Clerk shall distribute mail for authorized UH barracks residents to the UH barracks buildings and place all correctly addressed mail and oversized mail notifications in the addressee’s receptacles, cluster boxes, or hold mail receptacles.
  • Special Circumstance Mail – The Mail Clerk will turn over undeliverable mail to the Directory Section on date of receipt. Mail received which cannot be identified to addressee shall be returned to sender.
  • Mail delivery – Mail Clerk shall deliver mail from the mail center to the JBPHH and Hickam UH barracks within one workday of receipt.
  • Key Control – The Mail Clerk shall establish and implement controls to ensure keys issued by the Government are not lost, copied, or misplaced, are not used by unauthorized person, and that violations are immediately reported to the Government.

Mandatory Qualifications:


  • Applicant must be a US citizen
  • Applicants MUST have a “SECRET” clearance in order to perform under this contract
  • Applicant must be proficient in using computers and programs such as Microsoft Word, Excel, and Outlook
  • Applicant must have a valid driver’s license
  • Applicant may be required to lift items up to 70 lbs
  • Applicant will be required to wear safety shoes
