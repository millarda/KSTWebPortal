Information Assurance Technician


 Ka’ala Systems Technology Corp. in in need of an Information Assurance technician to support the Tactical Telecommunications Support contract in Port Hueneme, CA. Prior Military experience is desirable.


Job Duties:


  • Authority to Operate (ATO) is an accreditation process which NECCPAC’s network systems must complete to maintain vital connections to the Global Information Grid(GIG).
  • Ability to maintain vital ATO accreditation without interruption at all times
  • Review guidance documentation on the ATO accreditation process regularly and ensure compliancy
  • Ability to review RMF, SAP, HBSS, and STIGs
  • Ability to validate HBSS configurations
  • Serve as Cyber Security Agent
  • Ensure confidentiality, integrity, and availability of systems, networks, and data through planning, analysis, development, implementation, maintenance, and enhancement of information assurance and policies
  • Ability to troubleshoot, administer, and configure HBSS product issues
  • Provide remote video teleconference (VTC) support
  • Serve as administrative support for as many as ten unclassified or classified VTC conferences daily to ensure VTC connectivity
  • Serve as primary point of contact for coordinating and scheduling VTC.
  • Provide support as an ESSC Electronic Key Management System local element user
  • Ensure implementation of Navy policies, directives, and procedures for proper handling and safeguarding of secure electronic encryption devices and cryptographic files

Minimum Requirements:


  • Must be a US Citizen
  • Must have AT LEAST ONE of the following certifications:
    • CAP
    • CASP CE
    • CISM
    • CISSP (OR ASSOCIATE)
    • GSLC
