KST Awarded US Navy contract August 2017 for Pacific Missile Range Facility Administrative Support Services


 Ka’ala Systems Technology Corp. Awarded US Navy contract August 2017 for Pacific Missile Range Facility Administrative Support Services. POP 11 Sep 2017 -10 Sep 2018.

 Ka’ala Systems Technology (KST) Corp. was awarded the Prime contractor for provide administrative and clerical assistance to the NAVSUP Code 207 Regional Contracting Detachment, Pacific Missile Range Facility (PMRF), Barking Sands, Kekaha, Kauai, HI. Duties are in support of a FLC PMRF Detachment contracting operation. The primary services involve providing direct support to the Director, PMRF Contracting Office and to the Contracting Officers/Specialists/Staff. In addition, serves as the receptionist to the Detachment.
