System Administrator (VMWare)


Location: Port Hueneme, CA


Job Type: Full-time


Mandatory Applicant Attributes:


  • U.S. citizenship certifications
  • Active TOP SECRET or SECRET security clearance
  • 1 Year VMWare experience


Benefits:


  • Medical and Supplemental Health Insurance
  • Cafeteria 125 Plan
  • 401k Plan
  • Life Insurance
  • 10 paid federal holidays AND 10 days of leave annually
  • $50,000 - $60,000 annually, DOE

General Duties:


  • VMware
  • Microsoft
  • Red Hat Linux (RHEL 5/6)
  • NetApp and NOS products
  • Systems Administrator will maintain the current systems operational and IA compliant
  • Update IT support staff on the latest technologies, tools, and processes
  • Administer & troubleshoot:
    • Network accounts, rights, and access to systems and equipment
  • Administer system resources, installation & integration of systems fixes & workarounds
  • Provide updates & enhancements, including:
    • Performance, capacity, availability, serviceability, and recoverability
    • Participates in special projects as required

Travel Required:


  • Travel may occur 1 – 3 times a year to Hawaii, Japan, Guam, or South Korea

Preferred Applicant Attributes:


  • Prior Military Tactical Telecommunications or System Administration experience is desirable
  • 2 years’ prior experience
  • CompTIA Security+ CE or the equivalent 8570 certifications

Skills:


  • Thorough knowledge & understanding of VMware products
  • Host-based Security Software
  • 3 years system administration experience
  • Understand network/systems design skills to include Server Virtualization, Network Security Design and Microsoft Systems Design
  • Experience with applying DISA STIGs
  • Troubleshoot and resolve IT problems across the enterprise.
  • Knowledge of various hardware platforms, including; servers, blade center solutions, storage area networks, SMTP and HTTP proxies
  • Tests, locates, and repairs equipment problems associated with trouble calls, and performs routine maintenance.
  • Maintains current knowledge of relevant technologies as assigned.
