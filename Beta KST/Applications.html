INSTRUCTIONS:

  1. Open Application for Employment documents (there are two required documents) by clicking on "Job Application Form" and "EEO Voluntarily Self-Identity" links at the bottom of this text.
  2. Save these Microsoft Word Documents to your computer. Open document with Microsoft Word or other text editing software
  3. You can fill out application on your computer or you can print it and then fill it out with black or blue ink.
  4. PLEASE TYPE OR PRINT. Complete the Job Application and EEO Self-Identity forms. You may attach a resume, but you must still complete all questions or else your application will be deemed incomplete and may not be considered. Please fill out each box (don't just indicate “See Resume.”) Completed Job Application forms are a Department of Labor requirement. Applications with missing or invalid job numbers will not be considered for any position.
