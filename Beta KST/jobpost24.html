Inside Plant Telecommunications Mechanic / Technician


 Ka'ala Systems Technology Corp. (KST) is looking for an Inside Plant Telecommunications Mechanic/Technician who can perform Inside Plant Premise Wiring, troubleshooting of various types of circuits and provide Technical Solutions for the 747 Communication Squadron located on Joint Base Hickam Pearl Harbor, Hawaii. The 747th Communications Squadron, is in charge of ensuring the delivery of communications and information capabilities to enable PACAF, 15th Wing (15WG), 67 tenant organizations and 15 geographically separated units (GSUs) to project peace and power throughout the Pacific and beyond.

Duties:


  • Install and maintain inside plant, cable types, and proper uses for voice, video, and data cables, interconnects, patch panels, and wall jacks.
  • Able to install required cable infrastructure; conduit, j-hooks, cable tray, ladder racks, telecommunication racks/cabinets, etc.
  • Terminate fiber and copper cable and install to its proper attenuation and performance levels per industry standards and testable measures.
  • Install shielded and unshielded copper and fiber optic solutions from high-density patch panels and cross-connect modules to outlets, cables, and accessories as required for network installation.
  • Install server racks of all sizes: open or with doors and sides, vented or mesh, with or without Plexiglas, and with all types of power strips or power distribution units (PDU)'s, fans, shelves, and various designs.
  • Perform first line troubleshooting activities for installed environments in conjunction with military personnel.
  • When a request is received, create a Technical Solutions and Costing (TS&C) when needed. Include it in the ticket with the request.

Required Skills and Education:


  • Active Government Secret Clearance
  • 5 years of Telecommunication Experience
  • Current CompTIA Security + certification
  • Must be able to lift or move items weighing up to 50 pounds and up to 100 pounds with assistance
  • Valid US civilian passport
  • Knowledge of Excel and Word Software
