Telecommunications Specialist


Location: Joint Base Pearl Harbor/Hickam Hawaii


 Ka’ala Systems Technology Corp. (KST) is looking for a Telecommunications Specialist who can work with a team to support the Navy Expeditionary Combat Command (NECC) located in Port Hueneme, CA and NECC Pacific (NECCPAC) located in Joint Pearl Harbor-Hickam. Prior Military experience is desirable, but not mandatory. Candidate will join a KST team and provide Telecommunications Services support NECC / NECCPAC and subordinate command Satellite Communications (SATCOM) by coordinating all related issues.

Skills:


  • Coordinate with requesting commands, circuit providers and others to ensure SATCOM is achieved.
  • Coordinate, test and monitor SATCOM connectivity.
  • Design satellite circuits and submit Satellite & Gateway Access Requests (SAR/GAR) for subordinate units.
  • Work with DISA, step sites and circuit solution providers to ensure most effective pathways.
  • Possess a working knowledge of troubleshooting radio frequency paths utilizing spectrum analyzers, Fireberds, and oscilloscopes.
  • Provide technical expertise on ground communications system operation & design. Plan and organize mission planning meetings and visit subordinate organizations to provide expert support for telecommunication programs.
  • Configure all Layer 2 and Layer 3 network services, to include; switches, routers, firewalls to Defense Information Systems Agency (DISA) Security Technical Implementation Guide (STIG) standards.
  • Manage delivery of network services provided via Government Furnished Expeditionary network systems. Services include; user authentication, Domain Name System (DNS), email, file storage, WEB access, Naval Messaging, and authorized software as documented per current approved applications list (provided SEPCOR).
  • Provide technical expertise for deployment of NECC / NECCPAC Large Scale Communication Asset (LSCA) for exercises or contingency environments per the Evolutions Tactical Communication Plan. Supporting field operations and theater support as required by events as directed by the local TA and COR.
  • Provide expert-level help desk assistance to Expeditionary Service Support Centers (ESSC) customers during normal duty hours and after hours as required per rotational shift while serving as the rotational “on call” technician. Service calls to be documented in monthly TA / COR reports.
  • Tactical communications systems require field support for software and operating system patching to maintain secure and functional networks.
  • Provide operator and maintainer support of tactical radio and satellite communication system.
  • Support NECC command elements in fleet concentration area of Joint Expeditionary Base Little Creek servicing EOD, Costal Riverine, Naval Logistics, and Naval Construction Forces ensuring forces deploy Tactical Networks and communication system and operators ready for mission tasking.

Travel:


 Travel per year may include 1-4 trips to Guam, 1-3 trips to Honolulu, 1 trip to Okinawa and 1 trip to South Korea.

Experience:


  • Two years of experience.


Mandatory Minimum Requirements & Certifications:


  • Security+
  • U.S. citizenship certifications.
  • Active DoD TOP SECRET security clearance
