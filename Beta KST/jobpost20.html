Network Administrator


 Ka’ala Systems Technology Corp. is in need of a Network Administrator to support the Defense Logistics Agency (DLA) by providing IT support services for DLA personnel at Joint Base Pearl Harbor Hickam, and remotely for other DLA personnel within the region. Prior Military Network Administrator experience is desirable.

Network Administrator Job Duties


  • Analyze customer requirement to ensure better contribution to development of networking tools.
  • Maintain all telecommunication tools and accessories such as servers and switches
  • Install, configure and provide support to all networking accessories in different workstations
  • Identify and resolve all hardware related problems for better performance and detect issues for networking strategies
  • Monitor systems to oversee firewall and other network related improvement processes
  • Ensure networking stability and monitor all work regularly
  • Work under general supervision developing the requirements of products from inception to conclusion
  • Develop required specifications for simple to moderately complex problems
  • Coordinate with the contractor’s project manager, COTRs, and Government user representatives to ensure accurate solutions and user satisfaction on technical matters

Basic Qualifications:


  • BS degree in an information technology related field, or 5 years of progressive Information Technology experience, and at least two projects in technical areas. At least one project shall have occurred within the past three years.
  • IT-I Level qualified
  • IAT-I Level qualified
  • CompTIA Security + (CE) or the equivalent IAW DoD 8570
  • Computing Environment Certification: CCNA (or higher) certifications
  • Valid driver’s license
  • Active Department of Defense Secret Clearance
  • US citizenship
