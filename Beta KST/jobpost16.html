Financial Analyst


Location: Joint Base Pearl Harbor/Hickam Hawaii


 The Financial Analyst position is ideal for detailed oriented person with prior Naval Service or prior Civil Service with vast experience in Financial Analytics. The idea candidate will need to thrive in a fast-paced environment, focused on providing exceptional Financial Analytics to our government customer. The successful candidate will be joining a staff of financial advisors devoted to superior client service and comprehensive financial planning—working closely with several senior advisors in the office.

Job Duties:


  • Proficient in utilizing Microsoft access/excel/outlook/share point/power point & Adobe Pro applications.
  • Proficient in exporting data from STARS & the defense travel systems for subsequent importing into various Microsoft applications to generate various financial exhibits.
  • Proficient in gathering/assembling/arraying/analyzing STARS & defense travel system data extracted to determine positive/negative financial trends w/the reporting thereof.
  • Proficient at analyzing STARS & defense travel system reports extracted to determine the accuracy/completeness thereof to include the ability to reformat employing Microsoft access/excel products.
  • Products to be produced on a daily/weekly/monthly basis: Un-submitted voucher report; approved status report; separation of duties report; debt management monitor report; account activity text report; account listing report; non-travel activity report; weekend/holiday activity report; declined authorizations report; delinquency report; DoD travel CBA aging analysis report; DoD travel IBA aging analysis report; travel card responses over 1%/2%; & agency program coordinator list.

Interpersonal Skills:


  • Strong Communication Skills - verbal and written
  • Intermediary problem analysis and problem-solving skills
  • Attention to detail and accuracy within documentation
  • Able to interact with Customer via phone, email and video teleconference

Requirements:


  • Must be a U.S. Citizen
  • Must possess and valid DoD Secret Clearance or higher
  • Must be proficient with Microsoft Office, Excel, and Adobe Acrobat.
