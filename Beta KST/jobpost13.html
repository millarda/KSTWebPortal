Network Administrator


 We are looking for a Network Administrator who can work with a team to plan the future growth and expansion of the Navy Expeditionary Combat Command (NECC) tactical network located in San Diego, CA. Prior Military Tactical Telecommunications experience is desirable.

Responsibilities:


  • The Network Administrator will be a part of a Network Support team responsible for maintaining the current systems operational, IA compliant and keeping the IT support staff updated on the latest technologies, tools and processes.
  • Provide monitoring and maintenance for NECC tactical and deployed expeditionary networks worldwide to ensure network connectivity, Information Assurance compliance, and proper resource allocations.
  • Candidate will be a part of the Network Support team responsible for the design and development of network components supporting the NECC Tactical Data Network.
  • Implementation, integration, and testing as well as support of the network development, documentation and configuration management adhering to the project level process plans.
  • Participates in special projects as required.

Skills:


  • Understand network architecture, engineering, design, implementation, and testing.
  • Tests, locates, and repairs equipment problems associated with trouble calls, and performs routine maintenance.
  • Maintains current knowledge of relevant technologies as assigned.
  • Troubleshoot Cisco Virtual Private Network
  • Knowledgeable and capable on Voice Over Secure Internet Protocol (VOSIP) / VOIP
  • Engage in regular network troubleshooting activities and the resolution network connectivity issues.
  • Understand network architecture, engineering, design, implementation, and testing.

Preferred Requirements:


  • 2 years’ experience
  • Prior Military Tactical Telecommunications or System Administration experience is desirable.
  • CompTIA Security+ CE or the equivalent 8570 certifications

Mandatory Requirements:


  • U.S. citizenship certifications.
  • Active TOP SECRET or SECRET security clearance.
